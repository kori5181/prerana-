About Prerana Educational and Charitable Trust

Established in the year 2011, Prerana Educational and Charitable Trust is a registered non-profit organization under the Indian Registration Trust Act of 1882. The trust is also recognized under Section 80G and Section 12A of the Income Tax Act, 1961, which allows donors to claim tax exemptions on their contributions. Additionally, Prerana is approved for Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) activities under the Companies Act, 2013, making it an ideal partner for organizations looking to fulfill their CSR obligations.

Driven by a humanitarian vision, Prerana Educational and Charitable Trust is committed to uplifting underprivileged communities and transforming the lives of homeless and destitute individuals. As a voluntary non-governmental organization (NGO), our primary mission is to bring about social and qualitative change in the lives of those in need by providing essential support, education, shelter, and other welfare initiatives.

We actively run various welfare and rehabilitation projects aimed at supporting homeless individuals, economically disadvantaged families, and marginalized sections of society. Our programs focus on areas such as education, healthcare, skill development, and food distribution, ensuring sustainable and long-term solutions for those in desperate need.

All donations made to Prerana Sewa Sansthan are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961, in India. We sincerely encourage generous contributions to support our noble cause.
